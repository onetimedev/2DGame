Elemental Guardian: A Return Home

The Story

You are set in a time of great anguish, monsters are attacking people in the night, and food is running low, many people are dying. There are some people helping fight back against the monsters, but they are severely outnumbered.
You start your journey as the Guardian who returns to his home after many years to find that it has been taken over by four elementalists that are controlling the monsters. It is your job to stop these nefarious elementals and find out who is behind them coming to your home.

How to play

You start off with no items, so you will have to find and open chests to get weapons to fight enemies. You wonâ€™t be able to fight an enemy until you get a weapon and put it in the highlighted slot. You have a maximum of 6 slots in your inventory to store weapons and if you die you will lose them all except the one in the highlighted box.
Walking up to an NPC will prompt some dialogue to pop up giving you little bits of the story. Walking up to an enemy will give you the option to fight or run away, choosing to fight will set a different screen view and you will engage in a real-time fight until there is a victor.
You will have to beat all the elemental bosses before you can fight the final boss, and once you defeat him you will have beaten the game, but you will still be free to explore the map.
